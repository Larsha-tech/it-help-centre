HOBB Network Drive Switcher

What Is This App?

The HOBB Network Drive Switcher is a secure tool that connects your computer to the correct network drive with a single click. Each team has their own password — you will only see the drive button that belongs to your team.

Available Drives

Button What it connects
M: Drive — Motif (Team Motif) Connects the Motif network folder as your M: drive. Only available to Team Motif after entering the Motif password.
B: Drive — Benz (Team Benz) Connects the Benz (shares) network folder as your B: drive. Only available to Team Benz after entering the Benz password.

How to Use

  1. Open the app — Double-click DriveSwitch.exe located at N:\Sys\Automation\DriveSwitch.
  2. Allow admin access — A Windows security prompt will appear asking "Do you want to allow this app to make changes?" — click Yes to continue. This is required to connect network drives.
  3. Enter your password — A password screen will appear. Type your team password and click Login (or press Enter). You will only see the drive button for your team.
  4. Connect your drive — Click your drive button. The status bar at the bottom will turn green and show "Success! Drive is connected."
  5. Close the app — Click the X button to close. Your drive will stay connected.
The app window will always stay on top of other windows until you close it. This is by design so you can always see the connection status.

Disconnecting Drives

Click the red Disconnect All Drives button to remove all mapped network drives from your computer. Drives are also automatically disconnected whenever you switch, so no manual cleanup is needed.

Troubleshooting

  • Windows UAC prompt appears — This is expected — click Yes. The app needs administrator rights to connect network drives on Windows 10 and Windows 11.
  • Wrong password message — Make sure you are using your team's password. Contact IT if you have forgotten it.
  • Status bar shows red error — Check that you are connected to the office network. Contact IT Support if the problem persists.
  • App does not open — Make sure you can reach the N: drive at N:\Sys\Automation\DriveSwitch. Contact IT Support for help.
  • Drive connected but not visible in File Explorer — Open File Explorer and press F5 to refresh. The drive should appear under "This PC".
