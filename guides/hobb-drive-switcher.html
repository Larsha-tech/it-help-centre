HOBB Network Drive Switcher

What Is This App?

The HOBB Network Drive Switcher is a small tool that connects your computer to the correct network drive with a single click. It automatically handles disconnecting any existing drive before connecting the one you need — no manual steps required.

Available Drives

Button What it connects
M: Drive — Motif Connects the Motif network folder as your M: drive. Any existing B: drive will be disconnected first.
B: Drive — Benz Connects the Benz (shares) network folder as your B: drive. Any existing M: drive will be disconnected first.

How to Use

  1. Open the app — Double-click DriveSwitch.exe located at N:\SysAutomation\DriveSwitch.
  2. Choose your drive — Click the blue M: Drive (Motif) button or the green B: Drive (Benz) button — whichever you need.
  3. Wait for confirmation — The status bar at the bottom will turn green and show "Success! Drive is connected."
  4. Close the app — Click the X button to close. Your drive will remain connected.
The app window will always stay on top of other windows until you close it. This is by design so you can always see the connection status.

Disconnecting Drives

If you need to disconnect all network drives, click the red Disconnect All Drives button. Both M: and B: drives will be removed from your computer.

Drives are also automatically disconnected whenever you switch — so if you click M: Drive while B: is connected, B: is removed and M: is connected in one step.

Troubleshooting

  • Status bar shows red error — Check that you are connected to the office network or VPN. Contact IT if the problem persists.
  • App does not open — Make sure you can reach the shared network folder at N:\SysAutomation\DriveSwitch. Contact IT Support for help.
  • Drive connected but not visible in File Explorer — Open File Explorer and press F5 to refresh. The drive should appear under "This PC".
